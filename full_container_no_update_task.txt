Within ETA_containers.xlsx, each sheet corresponds to ETA months this year.

How to find corresponding container no? 2 tell-tale clues.
a)	The last digits of existing container no in GM/import_stock_receive_Jul23_1015am.xlsx should match in ETA_containers.xlsx

They don’t need to be an exact match. But the country, fruit type and fruit variety should at least be the same.

Only when criteria a) AND b) are fulfilled can we confidently know which full container no to assign to.
Finally, update the container no by keeping the prefix GM- intact. And then appending the full container no to it. E.g. if TEMU 925 0900 is the full container no in ETA_containers.xlsx, then the respective item should be GM- TEMU9250900. Notice how I removed the white spaces.
